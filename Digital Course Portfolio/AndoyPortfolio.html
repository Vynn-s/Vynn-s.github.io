Crave Compass

Crave Compass is a portable application designed and developed by MA Solutions. Its intended purpose is to provide extensive data regarding food retail, services, and locations based on a user’s preferences or current food trends. This is to address the lack of an existing single platform that covers these criteria, which often becomes a problem for customers.

Worker Management System

The Worker Management System is a Java-based application designed to efficiently manage worker records, including personal details, job roles, and salaries. It allows administrators to add, update, and remove worker information while providing a structured way to track work assignments and payroll. The system enhances organization and simplifies workforce management through an intuitive interface and database integration.

Dwelly

Dwelly is a C# Windows Forms application with SQL integration that allows users to recommend residences for rent or living. It provides a platform where users can share details about available apartments, including location, pricing, and owner contact information. Other users can browse recommendations to find suitable places in their area, making house-hunting easier and more community-driven.
